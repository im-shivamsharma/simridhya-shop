KAHANI SUNO

Bridging Heritage with Contemporary Silhouettes

At Simrdhya, we believe that fashion is a canvas for storytelling. Our brand is born from a deep love for Indian folk arts, specifically the bold, energetic visual vocabulary of Indian Truck Art.

Historically a medium for truckers to display their hometown pride, religious devotion, and poetic wit, truck art represents the unfiltered soul of India's pop culture. We extract its most stunning elements—vibrant colors, clean hand-painted motifs, and decorative borders—and blend them into premium contemporary clothing.
